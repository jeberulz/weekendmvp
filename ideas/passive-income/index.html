Passive Income App Ideas

Products that generate revenue with minimal ongoing effort. Set it up once and let it run. These ideas are designed for low-maintenance operation with high automation potential.

Total:5 ideas < 5 hours/week maintenance

What Makes an Idea "Passive"?

Truly passive products are rare, but low-maintenance is achievable. Look for: one-time purchases, subscription products with low churn, and automated delivery. The key is building systems that run without constant attention—automated onboarding, self-service support, and minimal feature updates.

High Automation

The product runs itself

Automated billing, delivery, and basic support. No manual intervention for standard operations.

Low Maintenance

< 5 hours per week

Occasional updates, minimal customer support needs, stable infrastructure.

Minimal Support

Self-service first

Clear documentation, FAQ, in-app guidance. Users solve most issues themselves.

Strategies for Passive Revenue

Subscription Model

Recurring revenue with low churn. Once customers are onboarded, they pay monthly without action.

  • Predictable monthly revenue
  • Automatic billing with Stripe
  • Focus on reducing churn, not sales

Affiliate Revenue

Build free tools that recommend paid products. Earn commissions on referrals.

  • No customer support needed
  • Scales with traffic (SEO)
  • Partner handles fulfillment

Usage-Based Pricing

Charge per API call, document processed, or action taken. Revenue scales with usage.

  • No manual invoicing
  • Aligns cost with value
  • Built-in usage caps prevent abuse

Digital Products

Templates, courses, or ebooks. Create once, sell forever. Zero marginal cost.

  • One-time creation effort
  • Automated delivery (Gumroad, Lemon)
  • 100% profit margin

Automation Checklist

Before launching, make sure these systems are automated:

Billing & Payments

Stripe handles subscriptions, invoices, and failed payments automatically

User Onboarding

Self-service signup, email verification, and in-app tutorials

Support Documentation

FAQ, help docs, and common issue guides reduce support tickets

Error Monitoring

Sentry or similar to catch issues before users report them

Email Sequences

Welcome emails, tips, and re-engagement sent automatically

Infrastructure

Vercel/Railway auto-scales, no server management needed
