Frequently Asked Questions

Are AI wrappers a viable business?

Yes, when you add genuine value beyond raw API access. Successful AI wrappers offer better UX, domain-specific prompts, workflow integration, and cost optimization. The key is solving a specific problem, not just providing generic AI access.

Which AI API should I use?

OpenAI (GPT-4) and Anthropic (Claude) are leaders for text. For specific tasks, consider specialized models. Start with one provider, and add fallbacks later. Cost matters—Claude and GPT-4 are similar in capability but pricing varies by use case.
