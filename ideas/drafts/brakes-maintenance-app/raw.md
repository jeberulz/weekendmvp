Brakes Maintenance Tracker App that Automates Reminders and Records for Vehicle Owners ([$1M+ ARR Potential])
Car owners often neglect regular brake maintenance, leading to safety risks and higher repair costs. The market for brake repairs has seen a significant growth with a 203% increase across the last few years, indicating a gap in proactive maintenance solutions.

Why This Is Perfect For You
Your UX and product design experience are pivotal in creating an intuitive user interface for the app, while your technical skills will help in the coding and early stage development.

The Solution
Develop a mobile app that allows vehicle owners to log brake maintenance, set reminders for inspections or service, and track repair history. The app could integrate with local repair shops to offer direct booking services and special promotions for users.

Target Market
The target market includes vehicle owners, particularly in the age group of 25-45, comprising over 50 million potential users. They are likely to pay for services that enhance vehicle safety and maintenance ease.

Business Model
Freemium model with a subscription for advanced features (e.g., predictive maintenance alerts), partnerships with automotive shops for lead generation, and in-app advertisements.
