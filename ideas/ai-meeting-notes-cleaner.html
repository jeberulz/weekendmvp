AI Meeting Notes Cleaner

Paste messy transcripts. Get clean action items.

The Problem

Meeting transcripts from Zoom, Google Meet, or Otter.ai are messy. They're full of filler words ("um", "like", "you know"), off-topic tangents, and unclear speaker attributions. Busy professionals spend 15-30 minutes manually cleaning up notes after every meeting just to extract the 3-5 action items that actually matter. That's hours lost every week.

The Solution

A simple web app where users paste their raw meeting transcript and instantly get back: a clean summary, extracted action items with owners, key decisions made, and follow-up questions. One click to copy or export.

Market Research

The meeting productivity space is massive. Otter.ai raised $50M+, Fireflies.ai has millions of users, and Notion AI is adding meeting features. But most tools focus on transcription, not cleanup. There's a gap for a simple, single-purpose tool that just cleans notes.

  • Professionals average 11-15 meetings per week (Microsoft Work Trend Index)
  • AI transcription tools have normalized transcript exports - raw material is abundant
  • Reddit threads show frustration with "cleaning up" Otter/Zoom notes

AI Prompts to Build This

Copy and paste these into Claude, Cursor, or your favorite AI tool.

1. Project Setup

Create a Next.js app with Tailwind CSS for an AI Meeting Notes Cleaner. The app needs: - A single page with a large text input for pasting transcripts - A "Clean Notes" button - An output section showing: Summary, Action Items, Decisions, Follow-ups - Copy to clipboard buttons for each section - Dark mode styling Use the App Router and keep it simple - no auth needed for MVP.

2. Core Feature

Add an API route that takes a meeting transcript and uses OpenAI (or Anthropic) to extract: 1. A 2-3 sentence summary 2. Action items with owners (if mentioned) 3. Key decisions made 4. Open questions/follow-ups Return as structured JSON. Include error handling for empty or too-short transcripts.

3. Landing Page

Create a landing page above the main tool that: - Has a headline: "Clean your meeting notes in seconds" - Shows a before/after example - Has an email capture form for the waitlist - Includes 3 benefit bullets - Uses modern, minimal dark styling The tool should be visible below (or unlocked after email).
