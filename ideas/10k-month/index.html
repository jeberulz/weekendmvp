How to Reach $10K/Month/Month

At $10K/month, you need: 200 customers at $50/month, 100 customers at $100/month, or 20 enterprise customers at $500/month. Consider moving upmarket or adding annual plans.

Volume Play

500 customers × $20/mo

Best for: Tools with broad appeal and low price points

Balanced Approach

200 customers × $50/mo

Best for: Niche SaaS and professional utilities

Premium Niche

100 customers × $100/mo

Best for: B2B tools and high-value workflows
