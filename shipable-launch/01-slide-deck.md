# ship·able — Slide Deck Script

Build in Pitch / Canva / Google Slides. Visual system mirrors the landing page: **ink black `#0a0a0a` backgrounds, cream `#fcfaf7`, warm orange accent `#e9a06a` / `#CC5500` / `#A03D00`,** big bold sans headline, one mono eyebrow per slide. Keep ~1 idea per slide. **[NOTES]** = what you say, not what's on the slide.

Total ~28 slides across the 90 minutes. Don't read slides — they're rails, the chat + live build is the show.

## SECTION 1 — Move 1: Sharpest idea (slides 5–10 · 00:10–00:33)

**Slide 5 — Move 1 divider (dark)**
> 01 — Your *sharpest* idea
> ~25 min · One buyer. One painful problem.

**Slide 6 — The cut**
> "Cool concept" → the **one specific thing** one specific person uses on day one.

**[NOTES]** The enemy is the idea cloud. We're not building the dream app. We're finding the single day-one use.

**Slide 7 — Three questions (the worksheet, on screen)**
> 1. Who is the **one** person? (name a real type, not "everyone")
> 2. What's the **one painful** thing they do today?
> 3. What would they use on **day one**?

**[NOTES]** Fill in YOUR example live, out loud, while they fill theirs. Narrate the cuts you make.

**Slide 8 — Bad vs sharp (example)**
> ❌ "An AI tool for freelancers."
> ✅ "A one-click invoice-chaser for solo designers who hate asking clients for money."

**[NOTES]** This contrast does the teaching. Make yours specific enough to feel uncomfortable.

**Slide 9 — Your one-liner (template)**
> *"[Tool] that helps [one person] do [one painful thing] without [the annoying way they do it now]."*

**[NOTES]** "Type your one-liner in chat now." React to 5–6 live. This is the engagement spike — use names.

**Slide 10 — Checkpoint**

## Design / production notes
- Export a **mobile-legible** version — half will watch on a second screen / phone.
- **Divider slides dark, content slides cream** — matches the brand and gives rhythm.
- Pre-load the **build tool + fallback URL** in separate tabs before you go live.
- Put the **Ship Sheet link + prompt pattern** as pinned chat messages so latecomers catch up.
- Keep a **timer widget** visible to you (not necessarily on slides).
