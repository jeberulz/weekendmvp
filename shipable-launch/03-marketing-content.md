## Notes on claims (read before you send/post)
- Keep **outcome promises** about the *deliverable* (a live URL, a build plan), not results (users/money). On owned channels (email/organic) you have more latitude than on Meta, but unverifiable income claims still erode trust and create refund/dispute risk.
- The "$738 value" stack is fine on the page; in email keep it light or it reads as a pitch.
- Every email/post = one clear CTA to `/shipable`. Add UTM tags (`?utm_source=email&utm_campaign=shipable_e1`, etc.) so you can see which channel drives qualified buyers.
