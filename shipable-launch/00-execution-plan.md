## 0. The one number that matters

Everything below optimizes for **qualified buyers at ≤ ~$20 CPA who show up live.** Not ticket revenue. The $9 exists to (a) filter tire-kickers out and buyers in, and (b) earn the click back so ads are ~free. Profit is on DARE Live ($29) and the program behind it.

Realistic targets for a first run with a small list + ~$300–1k ads over 12 days:
- **Seats sold:** 60–120 (blended; ads do most of the lifting given small list)
- **Live show rate:** 40–55% → ~30–60 live
- **Shipped-on-call:** ≥ 60% of live → your first *real* testimonials
- **ship·able → DARE:** ≥ 15% of buyers

If you only hit 40 seats, that's still a win: it's 40 qualified buyers + a stack of real proof for the next run, which is cheaper because you stop running on fiction.

## 3. Run-of-Show — the live 90 minutes (+ ~15 Q&A)

The page promises three moves (≈25 / 30 / 35 min). Here's the minute-by-minute that delivers a deployed URL without the call derailing. **Golden rule: you build a real example live, they build theirs in parallel in the Ship Sheet. Everyone deploys *something* before they leave.**

| Time | Block | What happens | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| 00:00–00:06 | **Open + promise** | Welcome, the "you never ship" frame, set the deal: "in 90 min you leave with a live URL. Not notes. A URL." Show your own screen, Ship Sheet open. | Energy + clarity on the deliverable |
| 00:06–00:10 | **Set the rails** | Everyone opens the Ship Sheet. Pick ONE idea. "If you have five, the workshop will pick for you in 4 minutes." Tool stack shown (the no-code/AI build tool you'll deploy with). | Everyone has one idea + the worksheet |
| 00:10–00:33 | **MOVE 1 — Sharpest idea (~23m)** | Cut idea → one buyer, one painful problem, one "day-one" use. You do it live on your example; they fill Section 1. 2-min "type your one-liner in chat" checkpoint — you react to 5–6. | Section 1 done; chat is alive |
| 00:33–01:03 | **MOVE 2 — Weekend cut (~30m)** | Scope to the *one screen that proves it*. Kill features out loud. You define your example's single screen + the single action a user takes. They fill Section 2. Checkpoint: "what's your one screen?" | Section 2 done; scope is tiny + real |
| 01:03–01:38 | **MOVE 3 — Live launch page (~35m)** | **The build.** You prompt the AI builder live → generate the page → deploy → paste the live URL in chat. Then they do theirs. You float, fixing in chat. The win moment: people pasting their own URLs. | Deployed URL each |
| 01:38–01:45 | **Capture-first-users + close** | Add the email-capture / waitlist to the page. "This is now validating demand. Send it to 5 people today." Then: the 48-Hour Build Plan + the two live bonuses (AI MVP Builder, Starter Kit) — how to claim. Tease DARE Live one sentence. | URL + a way to capture users + next step |
| 01:45–02:00 | **Q&A + screenshot moment** | Live Q&A. Ask everyone to drop their URL in chat → screenshot it (your future proof + social). | Testimonial/proof raw material |

**Facilitation rules:**
- **Pre-build a fallback.** Have a finished version of your example URL ready to paste if the live build chokes. Never let the room watch you debug for 6 minutes.
- **Time-box hard.** A visible timer per move. When it dings, move on — stragglers catch the replay.
- **Chat is the engine.** Every checkpoint = "paste it in chat." Momentum is social.
- **One co-pilot if possible.** A second person answering chat questions so you can present. Even a friend.
- **Record everything** — this is the replay (a paid deliverable) and your clip mine.

## 5. Decision rules (so you don't fiddle)

- **Ads, daily:** if a creative's CPA > $25 after $40 spend and 1k impressions → kill it. If < $12 → 20–30% budget bump. Don't touch winners more than once/day.
- **Seats pacing:** if at T-4 you're under 30 seats, shift budget to retargeting + email harder; don't add new cold creative (no time to mature).
- **Live build risk:** if rehearsal runs > 8 min to deploy, simplify the example until it's < 5. The room's build must be *boringly reliable.*
- **Refund stance:** instant, no questions, on a $9 product. Protects ad account + reputation. Cheaper than a dispute.
