Idea of the Day: AI Email Reply Drafter

Searches for 'automated email response examples' just jumped 238%. Here's the weekend build that rides the wave.

Idea of the Day

Searches for "automated email response examples" just broke out — up 238% to 2,900/month. Translation: knowledge workers are hunting for pre-baked replies because their inbox is winning. The template market is saturated. What's missing is a tool that writes the reply in your voice, not somebody else's.

AI Email Reply Drafter

Email eats 28% of the average workweek. Paste in the message you got, jot the 2–3 points you want to cover, pick a tone (professional, friendly, firm, apologetic), and get a polished reply back. Ship a voice-learning layer as the paid upgrade — trained on 5–10 of your own sent emails — and the output stops sounding like every other AI tool on the market.

Why it's a weekend build: ~6h to MVP, $1K/mo target. Stack: Cursor, Claude, and Bolt.
