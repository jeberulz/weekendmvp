## Keyword Intent Mapping

### Informational Intent
- "what is", "how to", "ideas for", "best"
- Target: Category pages, Tool pages, Audience pages, Problem pages
- Goal: Education and discovery

### Commercial Intent
- "$X/month", "make money", "revenue", "business ideas"
- Target: Revenue pages, Category pages with revenue focus
- Goal: Conversion to starter kit or consultation

### Navigational Intent
- Branded searches, specific tool names
- Target: Tool pages, Homepage
- Goal: Direct traffic
