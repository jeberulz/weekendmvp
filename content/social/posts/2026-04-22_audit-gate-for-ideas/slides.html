LESSONS
Three lessons from building the gate.
Gate beats vibes
Manual review misses gaps. A gate doesn't. Every page either PASSes the thresholds or gets blocked from merge until it does.
Status beats score
STUB / THIN / PASS beat a 0–100 number. A status tells you what to do next — a score leaves the decision to you.
Bulk beats solo
Fixing one page is a chore. Eight is a sprint. The gate turns scattered follow-ups into one focused session.
