07 / 08
WHAT WORKED
Three moves I'd repeat on day one.
01
Charged on day 1
No free trial. No freemium. $29/mo from the first click. Filtered for people who actually have the pain.
02
Shipped the teardown before the tool
LinkedIn carousel explaining the idea drove 48% of customers. The landing page was the teardown.
03
Built for one persona, not three
Sales reps only. Skipped the "also works for X" copy. Laser focus meant every post landed.
