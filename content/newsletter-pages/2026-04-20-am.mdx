Something just shifted in the job market. "Recruit me" searches jumped **10,178%** this month to 60,500/mo — job seekers are hunting for tools that position them to get hired, not just spruce up their resume. Meanwhile, 75% of resumes are still rejected by ATS bots before a human ever sees them. Clean wedge.

### AI Resume Tailorer

Job seekers know they should tailor their resume for each application, but it's tedious work — most blast the same generic PDF and wonder why they never hear back. AI Resume Tailorer fixes that: upload your resume once, paste any job description, and get back a version rewritten for that specific role with an ATS compatibility score and keyword suggestions.

**Why it's a weekend build:** ~8h to MVP, $1K/mo target. Stack: Cursor, Claude, and Bolt.
