Automate Customer Support

Handle more support tickets without hiring more people. Build AI-powered tools that answer common questions, triage issues, and escalate when needed.

3 solution concepts 70% of queries are repetitive

The Problem

Customer support doesn't scale linearly. As your product grows, so does the volume of support requests—but most of them are asking the same 20 questions. Small teams get overwhelmed, response times suffer, and founder time gets eaten up answering "how do I reset my password?" for the hundredth time. Meanwhile, customers expect instant responses, 24/7.

Repetitive Questions

70%+ of support tickets ask the same questions that have documented answers.

Response Time Pressure

Customers expect sub-hour responses but teams can't staff 24/7.

Scaling Challenges

Hiring support agents is expensive and doesn't solve the root problem.

Solution Ideas

AI Tools ~10-12 hours to build

AI Support Chatbot

A chatbot trained on your documentation, FAQ, and past support tickets that handles tier-1 support questions automatically. When it can't answer confidently, it creates a ticket and routes to a human.

RAG-Powered Smart Escalation 24/7 Coverage
Automation ~8-10 hours to build

Support Ticket Classifier

AI that automatically categorizes, prioritizes, and routes incoming support tickets. Identifies urgent issues, assigns to the right team member, and suggests relevant documentation.

Auto-Tagging Priority Detection Smart Routing
Productivity ~6-8 hours to build

Response Suggestion Engine

AI that drafts responses for support agents based on the ticket content and your knowledge base. Agents review, edit if needed, and send—cutting response time by 60%+.

Draft Generation Human in Loop Tone Matching

Quick Start Guide

  1. 1

    Collect your knowledge sources

    Gather FAQs, documentation, help articles, and past support conversations.

  2. 2

    Build a vector store

    Chunk and embed your content using OpenAI embeddings + Pinecone/Supabase pgvector.

  3. 3

    Create the chat interface

    Build a simple chat widget that queries your knowledge base and generates responses.

  4. 4

    Add escalation logic

    Detect when the AI is uncertain and seamlessly hand off to a human agent.
