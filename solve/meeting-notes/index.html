Automate Meeting Notes

Transform messy meeting transcripts into clean, actionable summaries using AI tools. Stop spending 15-30 minutes per meeting manually extracting action items.

2 solution ideas 11-15 meetings per week average

The Problem

Meeting transcripts from Zoom, Google Meet, or Otter.ai are a mess. They're full of filler words, off-topic tangents, and unclear speaker attributions. Professionals spend 15-30 minutes after every meeting manually extracting the few action items that actually matter—that's hours lost every week on busywork that AI can do in seconds.

Time Drain

15-30 minutes per meeting spent on manual note cleanup and action item extraction.

Information Chaos

Raw transcripts mix important decisions with small talk and filler words.

Lost Accountability

Action items get buried and forgotten when they're not clearly extracted and assigned.

Solution Ideas

Productivity ~8 hours to build

AI Meeting Notes Cleaner

A simple web app where users paste their raw meeting transcript and instantly get back a clean summary, extracted action items with owners, key decisions made, and follow-up questions.

Paste & Process Action Items One-Click Export
Productivity ~10 hours to build

Automated Documentation Tool for Remote Teams

A comprehensive tool that captures meeting notes, decisions, and action items automatically, then organizes them into searchable documentation for async remote teams.

Auto-Capture Team Collaboration Searchable Archive

Quick Start Guide

  1. 1

    Choose your approach

    Decide between a simple paste-and-process tool or an integrated team documentation system.

  2. 2

    Set up AI processing

    Use OpenAI or Anthropic APIs to extract summaries, action items, and decisions from transcripts.

  3. 3

    Build the input interface

    Create a simple paste area or integrate with Zoom/Meet for automatic transcript capture.

  4. 4

    Add export options

    Let users copy to clipboard, export to Notion/Slack, or save as structured documents.
